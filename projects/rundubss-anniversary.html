Run Dubss | 1st Anniversary Campaign

Dublin, 2026

Visual identity for a milestone moment.

We developed a complete promotional campaign for the anniversary of Dublin's first Spanish-speaking running club. From banner design to the event's summary film. The result is a energetic visual narrative that honours the club's community spirit and cultural uniqueness, blending motion and graphic design.
